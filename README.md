# 📊 Business Insights 360 – Veloxis Dashboard

An end-to-end Power BI dashboard designed to analyze business performance across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive levels.

---

## 🚀 Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw business data into actionable insights through an interactive and user-friendly dashboard.

The goal was not just to visualize data, but to **enable decision-making through storytelling and structured analytics**.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- 🌑 **Custom Dark-Themed UI (Veloxis Design)**
- 📌 **Insight-driven storytelling banners**
- 🔄 **Dynamic toggle (Market vs Segment analysis)**
- 📈 **Revenue → Profit journey (Unit Economics)**
- 📊 **Interactive KPI cards with variance tracking**
- 🧭 **Structured navigation across multiple business views**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Views

### 1. Home / Landing Page
- Central navigation hub
- Overview of key business sections

### 2. Finance View
- Profit & Loss statement
- Net Sales, Gross Margin, Net Profit analysis
- Region & segment breakdown

### 3. Sales & Marketing View
- Customer & product performance
- Profitability matrix (NS vs GM%)

### 4. Supply Chain View
- Forecast Accuracy
- Net Error & Risk profiling

### 5. Executive View
- High-level KPIs
- Revenue distribution & market share trends

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Revenue increased significantly post-2020, but **profitability declined due to rising operational costs**
- High-performing segments drive revenue but face **margin pressure**
- Forecasting trends highlight **volatility and inefficiencies in supply chain planning**
